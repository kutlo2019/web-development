STBK Business Services

Services

We assist students with their student tier-4 visas. This visa needs to be applied for 6 months before the start of the course. We also help students obtain express visa, which can take up to 2 weeks.

Visa Applications

Students are assisted to get their tier 4 visa to start their study in the Uk. This includes assisting with payments to be made for the VISA.

Guidance and Counselling

When it's time for considering career options, students can face difficult choices to make. Their strengths and weeknesses would be assessed so as they can be assisted in making informed decisions.

UCAS Applications

The applications are done online through Universities and Colleges Admissions Services (UCAS) and STBK is an officially registered with UCAS as an application centre and students adviser.

Expositions

We invite UK universities to come, meet and introduce themselves and their services to the students eligible for sponsorship. We are the official representatives of several UK universities.

Follow Ups

In instances where students encounter challenges with the universities set conditions we intervene on their behalf. ( eg EFL requirements, deposit payments & accommodation, etc.).

Pre Departure Briefing

We organize pre-departure briefing sessions, at the sessions we invite guests from different organisations to motivate & address the students on issues concerning various aspects of life in the UK.
